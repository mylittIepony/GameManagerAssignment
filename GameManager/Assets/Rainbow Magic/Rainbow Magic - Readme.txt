------------------
Rainbow Magic by Archanor VFX
------------------

The effect prefabs can simply be dragged and dropped into the scene, or spawned with a script.

Feel free to reference or re-purpose any of the included scripts for your games or projects.

If you upgrade this asset to URP, keep in mind that Additive materials may look slightly different compared to the Built-in Render Pipeline.

------------------
Common Issues (Invisible Particles)
------------------

* If your particles are invisible or don't render in a build, try turning off Soft Particles.
